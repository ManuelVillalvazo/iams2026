What is IAMS?

A humanitarian medical academy with a global mission.

The International Academy of Medical Specialists, Inc. brings physicians together to strengthen medical education, professional standards and humanitarian service.

Founded in 2006

IAMS was formed by physicians who believed medical knowledge should serve both professional excellence and public good.

Mission

A medical organization built on service, education and social responsibility.

IAMS was created to bring together medical specialists, public health advocates and professional leaders who share a commitment to better healthcare, continuing education and humanitarian service. Its work is guided by integrity, innovation, adaptability, altruism and social responsibility.

01

Medical Education

IAMS supports continuing education and professional development for physicians and medical specialists.

02

Humanitarian Outreach

The organization collaborates with civic groups and partners to support medical missions and outreach programs for underserved communities.

03

Global Collaboration

IAMS creates an avenue for Filipino doctors, international physicians and medical professionals to share knowledge across borders.

Philanthropy

Medical missions that bring care directly to patients.

IAMS congresses and professional gatherings are connected to a broader humanitarian purpose: supporting medical missions, free consultations and healthcare outreach for communities that need direct access to medical professionals.

Why this matters for speakers.

Contributing to the IAMS World Congress is not only an academic contribution. It is a way to support a humanitarian medical platform that uses knowledge, professional relationships and public service to extend care beyond the conference room.

Core Values

Professionalism with a humanitarian purpose.

IAMS emphasizes the values that support public trust in physicians and medical practitioners: integrity, innovation, adaptability, altruism, social responsibility and the commitment to make a difference.
